About us

Inova is our newly formed company. Our business is focused around frugal innovation and we wish to spread our wonderful experience with theis subject with people all around the world. We want to acomplish this by hosting innovation camps.

Frugal innovation is the process of reducing the complexity and cost of certain goods and its production. Usually this refers to removing nonessential features from durable goods, such as a car or phone, in order to sell it in developing countries.
